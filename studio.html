Everything on this site annotates other people's sentences. This page turns the tools on yours. Mark who's naming, who's acting, what's describing — then look at the colors: Is every sentence opening in the same place? Is anything situating your kernels, or are they bare? Do your dependent clauses put the background where you meant it?
